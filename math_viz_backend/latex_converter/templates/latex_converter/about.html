About

This tool was created to solve the problem of turning messy or handwritten math into clean, professional-looking equations. Whether you're a student, teacher, or anyone working with formulas, this tool lets you convert messy math text into polished images and LaTeX code. Then, you can either copy the output image and paste it into your document, or copy the LaTeX code and insert it using LaTeX-compatible editors.

What it Does

The app offers three easy ways to convert math into visuals:

  • Text to Math:

    • Type in your math in plain, unformatted text: x=(-b+-sqrt(b^2-4ac))/2a. The tool converts it into a formatted math image and gives you the LaTeX code behind it.

  • Drawing to Math:

    • Sketch your equation by hand using your mouse or touchpad. The app will interpret your drawing and turn it into a clean math image + LaTeX.

  • Upload Image to Math:

    • If you have a picture or a screenshot of math text, upload it or paste it in. The tool processes the image and gives you a clean version of the equation along with the LaTeX.
